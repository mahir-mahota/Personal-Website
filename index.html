About Me

I'm a second year Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. Motivated by curiosity, I started experimenting with electronics and robotics at a young age. From there grew a strong interest in all things related to embedded software and machine learning. My name translates to 'skillful' and this is a word I try to embody in my daily life by always striving to learn and grow. I'm currently working as a co-op Firmware Developer at onsemi. Outside of work, I like playing squash, reading and speedcubing. I always enjoy working on new projects and you can find out all about them below.
